About Me

My first experiences with designing stemmed from graphic design and media arts, where my younger self played around with fonts, colours, and layouts to create collages of my favourite characters. I was inspired by others who posted amazing video edits on Instagram, motivating me to self-learn After Effects, in hopes of creating similar content. Being a creator was a very enjoyable process and I had a fun time collaborating with others around my age with the same interests as me. I found myself excited whenever I was able to apply these skills at school, whether it was editing my elementary school graduation video or crafting aesthetic pleasing presentations.

Now, as a 3rd year design student enrolled in SFU's SIAT, I draw upon my background and employ empathy to design interactions that resonate with users' specific needs. I have experience with designing mobile apps and desktop websites, and I'm constantly looking for opportunities to expand my skillset and grow as a designer.
